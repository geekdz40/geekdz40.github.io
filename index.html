// About Us

Who We Are

GeekDZ is Abbas Laghrour University's official technology club — a community built by students, for students. We promote technical culture and develop real-world skills across programming, cybersecurity, AI, blockchain, and scientific research.

As an official Red Hat Academy partner, we give our members access to industry-grade certifications and learning paths. We don't just study technology — we build it, compete with it, and publish it.
