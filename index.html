About Us

GeekDZ is an active technology club at Abbas Laghrour University in Khenchela, aiming to promote technical culture among students and develop their skills in programming, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, scientific research, blockchain, and modern technology.

We strive to create an interactive learning environment by organizing workshops, competitions, and group projects that help students gain practical experience and build a strong professional network.
